Now we need to find\\ $\pr{X=2,Z=1|X=1,Z=1}$= a ( let )
\\From conditional probability we have
\begin{align}
    a=\frac{\pr{X=1,X=2,Z=1}}{\pr{X=1,Z=1}}
\end{align}
\begin{multline}
    \pr{X=1,Z=1}=\\
    \sum_{i=1}^{3}\pr{X=1,Z=1|Y=i}
    \times \pr{Y=i}
\end{multline}
