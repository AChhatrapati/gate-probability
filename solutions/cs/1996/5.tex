Probability of at least one six facing up
    \begin{align}
         & = {Pr(X = 1)} + {Pr(X = 2)} \\
         & = {^2 C _1}{p}{q} + {^2 C _2}{p^2}{q^0}\\
         & = {^2 C _1} \left( {\frac{1}{6}}\right) \left( {\frac{5}{6}}\right) + {^2 C _2} \left(\frac{1}{6}\right) ^2\\ 
         & = 2 \left(\frac{5}{36}\right) + \frac{1}{36}\\
         & = \frac{11}{36}
    \end{align}
