Let $X = \cbrak{10,11, \ldots, 99}$ be a random variable. Here, $\floor*{x}$ rounds off $x$ to the greatest integer less than $x$.
\begin{align}
    \pr{X \tpmod{7}\neq 0} &= 1 - \frac{n(X \tpmod{7}= 0)}{n(X)}\\
    &= 1 -  \frac{\floor*{\frac{100}{7}}- \floor*{\frac{10}{7}}}{90} \\
    &= 1 - \frac{13}{90}\\
    &= \frac{77}{90}
\end{align}
